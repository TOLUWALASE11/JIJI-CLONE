WEBSITE LAYOUT

A layout can simply be defined as the arrangement of things
A web layout on the other hand, is considered as the neat and ordered arrangement of objects on a webpage.
A nice web layout would ensure your website is nicely arranged and visually appealing.
A website layout is a pattern (or framework) that defines a website's structure.
It has the role of structuring the information present on a site both for the website's owner and for users.
It provides clear paths for navigation within web pages and puts the most important elements of a website front and center.

Features/layout of a basic webpage

A good web layout consists of header, content and footer

header:this refers to the top most part of a webpage or the introductory content
of the webpage and usually contains the topic of a website and equally serves as a container
for navigational aids to other webpages

content:this refers to the content of the webpage.the article represents a complete
or self contained composition in a page, document or site that can be shared or reused.
the article can be a forum post, a magazine, a blog entry, a comment, an interactive
widget or any other form of content.

footer:this is the bottom of a webpage or the sectioning root element.
A footer typically contains information about its section such as who wrote it
links to related documents, copyright data and the likes
